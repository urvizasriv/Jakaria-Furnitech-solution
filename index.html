Our Story

They say office is where the heart is. Taking this further, we say the heart is office wherein all your furniture is made to cherish. We; at Jakaria , have been at the forefront of the industry creating beautiful spaces for you within your offices. With a successful start by importing our furniture we came up with our own factories that manufactures office chairs , tables and almirah . We are humbled to have touched the lives of millions of people with our range of world class office furnitures. From being the pioneers in veneer business to being the leading furniture company and a lifestyle brand we have come a long way since then and the journey, so far, has been an enriching and memorable one.
